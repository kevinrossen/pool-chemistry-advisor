# Privacy Policy

**Pool Chemistry Advisor (TFP)**
Last updated: March 18, 2026

## Overview

Pool Chemistry Advisor is a free, open-source AI tool that provides pool water chemistry recommendations using the Trouble Free Pool (TFP) methodology. This privacy policy applies to the ChatGPT Custom GPT version of Pool Chemistry Advisor.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or process any personal data.** Pool Chemistry Advisor has no backend, no database, no analytics, and no user accounts.

## Pool Math API

When you provide your Pool Math share key, the GPT fetches your pool chemistry data directly from the Pool Math API (`api.poolmathapp.com`). This data is:

- Fetched in real time during your conversation
- Used only within your current ChatGPT session to generate recommendations
- Not stored, logged, or transmitted anywhere by this GPT

Your Pool Math share key and pool data are handled entirely by ChatGPT (OpenAI) and the Pool Math API. We have no access to your conversations, share keys, or pool data.

## Third-Party Services

This GPT interacts with the following third-party services:

- **OpenAI / ChatGPT** — Hosts and runs the GPT. Subject to [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy).
- **Pool Math by Trouble Free Pool** — Provides pool chemistry data via its public share API. Subject to [Trouble Free Pool's Privacy Policy](https://www.troublefreepool.com/help/privacy-policy/).

**Pool Chemistry Advisor is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by OpenAI or Trouble Free Pool.**

## Your Choices

- You can use Pool Chemistry Advisor without providing a Pool Math share key — just type your test results manually.
- Your Pool Math share key is optional and only used if you provide it.
- You can disable sharing in the Pool Math app at any time to revoke API access to your pool data.
